Architecture

MovenRun is three layers working as one: an effortless app, a verification layer that proves movement, and an onchain protocol on Base that settles ownership and value.

The three layers

What each layer does
LayerRole
ExperienceThe mobile app and its embedded wallet: onboarding, movement sessions, the territory map, clubs, and the game — with all crypto handled invisibly.
VerificationTurns a recorded route into a validated, deterministic proof of movement and attests it via the oracle, so onchain actions can trust real-world activity.
ProtocolThe onchain contracts on Base: the $MOVE token, Zone Deeds, challenges, seasons, the staking vault, and governance — the system of record for ownership and value.

How activity becomes ownership

  1. A player moves; the app records the route and requests a proof.
  2. The verification layer validates the route and produces a signed proof of movement.
  3. The oracle relays that attestation onchain.
  4. Protocol contracts settle the result — capturing a zone, minting $MOVE, updating a deed — under the player's keys.

Design principles

  • Onchain source of truth. Ownership and value live in contracts, not in an app database the operator can rewrite.
  • Trust-minimized inputs. The chain never takes movement on faith; it acts on verified proofs.
  • Invisible UX. Embedded wallets and sponsored gas keep the power of onchain without the friction.
  • Built on Base. Low fees, fast confirmation, and consumer-grade tooling make a mainstream onchain game viable.
