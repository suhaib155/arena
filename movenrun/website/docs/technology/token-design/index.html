$MOVE economy

$MOVE is the asset that powers MovenRun. It's minted by movement, demanded by utility, and tightened by sinks — an economy engineered to reinforce itself.

This page describes the $MOVE economic design. It is not financial advice, an offer, or a promise of returns, and it quotes no price or yield figure. Parameters are set and adjusted through governance and may evolve. See Legal & disclaimers.

The economic loop

How $MOVE enters

$MOVE is minted by verified movement — real, proven activity, never a faucet. Emission follows a capped, decaying schedule: issuance is highest early and steps down over time, so the network can bootstrap without inflating forever. Per-period limits prevent any single account from dominating issuance.

Why $MOVE is wanted

Demand comes from utility woven through the game, not from speculation:

  • Territory. Claiming premium zones, upgrading, and fortifying use $MOVE.
  • Competition. Entering challenges and seasonal contests requires it.
  • Staking. $MOVE can be staked in the vault to back the network and participate in its economy.
  • Governance. Holding and staking $MOVE gives a voice in how the network runs.

Sinks and balance

Several core actions burn $MOVE — permanently removing it from supply — so that as activity grows, supply tightens rather than dilutes. Zone taxes route value to active deed owners, and periodic seasonal mechanics reduce supply on schedule. The guiding rule is utility and sinks before incentives: the economy is designed to have reasons to exist beyond emissions.

Ownership on top

Zone Deeds are the ownership layer that sits above the token: active holders accrue standing and in-game yield, tying long-term participation to long-term ownership. Movement, token, and territory are designed as one reinforcing system.

Economic principles

  • Minted by proven activity, never by unverified claims.
  • Capped and decaying issuance, with anti-concentration limits.
  • Sinks and caps designed before incentives, for durability over hype.
  • Parameters governed onchain by the community, transparently.
