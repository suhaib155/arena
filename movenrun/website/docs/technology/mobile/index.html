The app

The MovenRun app is where the network meets the street: instant onboarding, live movement sessions, a rich territory map, and a game that feels native while settling value onchain.

Instant onboarding

Getting in takes a tap. An account and an embedded wallet are provisioned in the background, and the player is moving within seconds — no install friction, no seed phrase, no purchase. The crypto exists from the first second; it just never announces itself.

Movement sessions

The session flow — start, move, finish, capture — is the core of the app. Foreground GPS records the route while distance, time, and pace stay glanceable, then the summary and capture moment turn the outing into territory. See Movement & proof of activity.

The territory map and game

Beyond sessions, the app is a full game: a living territory map of your holdings and their state, per-zone detail views, progression and collections, clubs and city systems, and the ownership surface where deeds and $MOVE come together. It's designed to feel like a premium mobile game, with the onchain layer working underneath.

A consumer-grade experience

  • Daylight Cartography — a bright, map-native design language built for clarity and delight.
  • Smooth, purposeful motion that guides attention without ever being required to understand the game.
  • Built for reach — responsive, accessible, and fast, so the network works on the devices people actually carry.
