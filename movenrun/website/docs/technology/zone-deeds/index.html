Zone Deeds

A Zone Deed is a digital territory record: a durable, transparent link between a committed player and a zone. Today it exists as an educational preview and a testnet experiment — nothing more.

Local preview (in-app showroom) · Experimental testnet (V1 contract) · Under review (V2 design)

The concept

Territory in MovenRun is earned by repetition — and for most players, the in-app record is all they will ever want. A Zone Deed is for the minority who want more durability: an on-chain record (an NFT whose ID corresponds to the zone's grid cell) carrying ownership history and provenance that no app database controls. Think “a plaque for your neighborhood”, not “an asset class”.

Where it stands today

  • In the app: the Deed Preview Showroom — educational, read-only, “Local preview · No wallet · No minting” on-screen.
  • On testnet: the V1 suite on Base Sepolia includes a zone-deed contract (ERC-721, one token per zone cell, with dormancy/reclaim mechanics). It is an experiment with documented defects — see Contract status — and no consumer flow touches it.
  • Under review: the V2 redesign fixes the deed contract's known issues (challenge-lock transfers, state cleanup on reclaim, signature binding). Source and tests only; not merged, not deployed.

What a Zone Deed is not

  • Not land. No legal title, no real-world right, no claim over any physical place.
  • Not required. The game never gates a mechanic behind holding one.
  • Not purchasable today. There is no mint flow, no marketplace, no price — and none is promised.
  • Not an investment. No value, yield, or appreciation is stated or implied, on any network.
