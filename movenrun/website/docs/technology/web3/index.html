Onchain, made invisible

MovenRun is fully onchain — and you'd never know it unless you wanted to. That combination is the whole strategy: real ownership, zero friction, everyone welcome.

Why onchain at all

Because ownership only means something if it's real. When your territory, your deeds, and your $MOVE live onchain under your keys, they're genuinely yours — portable, provable, and outside any single company's control. That's what turns a movement game into a movement network: an open economy players own, rather than a closed app they rent.

Why you never see it

Onchain has historically meant seed phrases, gas tokens, network switching, and signature prompts — a wall most people never climb. MovenRun removes the wall:

  • Embedded wallets. A secure wallet is created for you at sign-up — no seed phrase to write down, no app to install.
  • Sponsored gas. Through account abstraction, transactions are paid for you. You never buy a gas token or think about fees.
  • Actions, not signatures. Complex onchain operations are batched behind ordinary game buttons.
  • One network. Everything happens on Base; there's nothing for you to configure.

Full control when you want it

Invisible doesn't mean locked in. Crypto-native players can connect an external wallet, export or take self-custody of their embedded wallet, and interact with their deeds and $MOVE like any other onchain asset. MovenRun meets you where you are: effortless for newcomers, fully open for power users.

Built on Base

MovenRun settles on Base, an Ethereum Layer 2. Base offers the low fees and fast confirmations a real-time game needs, a strong account-abstraction ecosystem for sponsored, wallet-light onboarding, and the security of settling to Ethereum. It's the chain that makes a mainstream onchain movement network practical.

The takeaway. MovenRun gives you real onchain ownership with the ease of a normal app. Newcomers get a game; the game quietly makes them owners.
