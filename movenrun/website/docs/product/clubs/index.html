Clubs, cities & competition

Alone you hold a neighborhood. Together you take a city. Clubs turn MovenRun into a living, citywide contest.

Clubs and crews

Join a club and your movement contributes to a shared cause: collective territory, combined district control, and a place on the club leaderboard. A Club Territory view ties your zones, districts, and rivalries into a single strategic picture, and crew missions turn that picture into weekly team goals worth chasing together.

District control and city wars

The city layer is where clubs collide. Districts are the prize; city wars are season-long battles to control them. Your daily movement is a move in a much larger game — the walk that fortifies one zone can be the difference in who holds a district when the season settles.

Rivals and pressure

The world pushes back. Rival players and clubs contest your territory, and unheld ground is an invitation. That pressure is what makes holding meaningful — a zone you've defended through a season is worth more than one nobody wanted.

Sponsored zones and events

The city layer can host sponsored zones and limited-time events — special areas that bring extra rewards, partners, and moments into the map. They're a way for real places and brands to become part of the game, and a way for the network to bring fresh objectives to a city.
