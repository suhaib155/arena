Privacy & your data

A movement network runs on location — which is exactly why MovenRun is built to prove your activity without hoarding your life.

Privacy-first by design

MovenRun's design goal is simple: keep as little sensitive data as possible, and turn movement into proofs rather than permanent trails. What the network needs is confidence that you really moved — not a searchable history of everywhere you've been. Verification is built around that principle.

What the network uses

  • Your route, to play. Location is read during an active session to draw your route and determine which zones you captured.
  • Proofs, to settle. Sessions are reduced to compact, deterministic proofs of movement; it's the proof — not a raw trail — that drives onchain capture.
  • Your holdings, onchain. Deeds and balances live onchain under your keys, as ownership records — not as a profile of your movements.

Your control

  • Your wallet and your deeds are yours; you can take full self-custody at any time.
  • The experience is built to minimize what's collected and to favor summaries over granular location data.
  • Data-retention and deletion controls are part of the network's design commitments as it scales.

What MovenRun is not

  • Not a data broker — your movement builds your assets, it isn't a product sold about you.
  • No third-party ad tracking, and no selling of location profiles.
  • No inference or exposure of other people's precise location.
