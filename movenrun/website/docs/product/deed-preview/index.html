Zone Deed preview

An educational showroom for a future idea: territory records you could one day choose to hold on-chain. Today it teaches; it does not mint.

Local preview — the Deed Showroom screen in the current app.

What the showroom is

The app includes a read-only Deed Preview Showroom: a gallery that explains, with example deed cards, what a Zone Deed could represent — a transparent digital record tying a committed player to a zone, with provenance and history. It exists so players can understand the concept long before deciding whether they ever want it. The screen is labeled with its status directly in the UI: Local preview · No wallet · No minting.

What it is not

  • No wallet — the app contains no wallet connection anywhere.
  • No minting — nothing can be created, claimed, or purchased.
  • No tradability — there is no marketplace, transfer, or price.
  • No ownership promise — viewing the showroom grants nothing and reserves nothing.
  • No real-world claim — a Zone Deed would be a digital game record, never land, property, or legal title.

What may become possible later

If the product reaches the point where transparent, portable territory records add real value for committed players, Zone Deeds would arrive through the gated path described in Zone Deeds (technology): experimental testnet first (a V1 exists on Base Sepolia today, with a V2 redesign under review), then independent audit, legal review, and product-readiness gates before anything reaches real users. Any of those steps can change or stop the plan.
