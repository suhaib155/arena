Progression & rewards

MovenRun rewards you two ways at once: a deep in-game progression you can feel every session, and a real onchain economy you actually own.

Levels, XP, and streaks

  • XP and levels track your overall journey and unlock capability as you climb.
  • Streaks reward consistency — the single strongest driver of progress in MovenRun. Show up, and everything compounds.
  • Daily and seasonal objectives give each day and each season a clear focus and payoff.

Movement points and $MOVE

Every session earns movement points that reflect your activity. As the onchain economy comes online, verified movement mints $MOVE — the network's asset — within a capped schedule, and your territory earns in-game yield while you actively hold it. Progression and ownership reinforce each other: the more consistently you move, the more you hold and the more your holdings do for you.

Collections, mastery, and seasons

  • Collections & badges turn territory milestones into a growing, ownable record of what you've achieved.
  • District mastery is the long arc — the neighborhoods you genuinely frequent become visibly, provably yours.
  • Seasons give the network a rhythm: recurring competitions with fresh objectives, standings, and rewards.

Progress you own

The difference between MovenRun and a typical fitness or game app is ownership. Your levels and streaks live in the game; your territory, your deeds, and your $MOVE live onchain, under your control. Progression here isn't a number a company can reset — it's an asset base you build.
