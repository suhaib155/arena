Movement & proof of activity

The session is the heart of MovenRun — and it's also where trust begins. Real movement becomes a proof the whole economy can rely on.

A session, start to finish

Tap to start and you're moving within seconds — no setup, no wallet prompt. The route draws live as you go, with distance, time, and pace kept simple and glanceable so your eyes stay on your surroundings. Pause and resume whenever you need. Finishing produces a clean summary and the capture moment that turns the outing into territory.

Why movement is proven

Because territory is a real asset, the game can't take movement on faith. Every session is validated for physical plausibility — realistic speed, sane accuracy, continuous motion — and reduced to a deterministic proof that the network's oracle attests. That proof is what lets a capture settle onchain. Effort is rewarded; fabrication is rejected. The full pipeline is in Movement verification.

Routines that build presence

  • Saved routes — your favorite loops, ready to run again and reinforce what you hold.
  • Streaks — consistency is the strongest force in the game; showing up compounds.
  • Seasons & objectives — recurring goals that give every week a focus and a reward.
  • Weekly recap — a clear read on your movement and how your territory changed.

Safety by design

  • Live UI is glanceable; nothing requires interaction while you move.
  • No mechanic rewards speed over safety — you never need to rush a crossing to hold a zone.
  • Move where it's safe and legal to move; the game is built around presence, not risk.
