The core loop

Move → Capture → Own → Grow. Four verbs that turn a walk into an asset — and an asset into a citywide presence.

Move

Walk, run, or cycle — all three are first-class. Your session records a route and turns it into a verifiable proof of movement. There are no minimum speeds and nothing to stare at mid-session; the game rewards showing up and covering ground.

Capture

Your route intersects zones on a global hex map, and finishing a session claims them. Capture is the moment a walk becomes a foothold on the map — the first step from moving to owning.

Own

Captured zones become Zone Deeds: onchain assets held under your keys. Ownership is the heart of MovenRun — a deed is yours to keep, strengthen, defend against challengers, and transfer. This is what separates MovenRun from every scoreboard: the map remembers you because you actually own part of it.

Grow

Ownership compounds. Zones form districts; districts build club and city standing; active holding earns $MOVE and in-game yield; and holding stake gives you a vote in the network. A year of morning walks becomes a portfolio of territory and a real voice in how the game runs.
