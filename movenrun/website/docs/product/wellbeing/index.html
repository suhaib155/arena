Movement for everyone

MovenRun is tuned so a 20-minute walk is a first-class move — because a network that only rewards athletes never changes how people actually live.

Every level, every mode

  • Walking, running, and cycling are equal — no mode is "better," and there are no minimum speeds.
  • Ownership grows from returning, not from raw pace or distance, so consistency beats intensity.
  • Daily goals fit into a lunch break; the strongest players are the most regular ones.

Accessible to everyone — including non-crypto users

MovenRun's biggest inclusivity decision is technical: by hiding wallets, gas, and jargon, it opens onchain ownership to people who would never make it past a typical crypto sign-up. No prior knowledge, no purchase, no setup — just movement. That's how a network reaches beyond the already-converted and becomes something anyone can join. See Onchain, made invisible.

Designed to be usable

  • Reduced motion respected across the experience; no information depends on animation.
  • High-contrast, readable UI with large touch targets and clear structure.
  • No color-only meaning — states pair color with labels and icons.
  • Interruptible — sessions pause; nothing punishes stopping to rest.

Move safely

Live sessions stay glanceable so your attention is on the world, not the screen, and no mechanic rewards risky behavior. Move where it's safe and legal to move. MovenRun encourages regular activity, which is generally healthy — but it is a game, not a medical service, and it does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition.
