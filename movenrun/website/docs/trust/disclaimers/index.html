Legal & product disclaimers

Plain statements, no fine print. These disclaimers apply to the MovenRun app, website, and all documentation pages.

Development-stage product

  • MovenRun is in active development. Features, availability, design, and timelines may change or be discontinued at any time without notice.
  • Preview systems use deterministic or fictional data — including clubs, rivals, leaderboards, sponsors, and events — and do not represent live services, real people, or real organizations.

No financial promises

  • Nothing in the product or documentation is financial advice, an investment solicitation, or an offer to sell anything.
  • MovenRun promises no rewards, earnings, income, yield, or returns of any kind. In-app progress (XP, streaks, movement points, the “Locked MOVE” preview counter) is game state — not money, not a token, not redeemable.
  • There is no token sale, no price, and no exchange listing, and none is announced or implied.

No real-world ownership

  • Zones, territory, districts, and Zone Deeds are digital game concepts. They confer no ownership, tenancy, easement, or any other right over real-world land or property, and imply no affiliation with any real place.

Testnet assets

  • Anything deployed on a test network (Base Sepolia) is an experiment. Testnet tokens and records have no value, no promised durability, and no promised migration to any other network.
  • The V1 testnet contracts have documented defects and must not be relied upon or interacted with in the expectation of sound behavior.

Health context, not medical advice

  • Statements about movement and wellbeing describe general product design. MovenRun does not provide medical advice and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. Consult a professional where appropriate, and move only where it is safe and legal.

Terms and privacy policy

  • These documentation pages are informational and are not terms of service or a privacy policy. Binding terms and a formal privacy policy will be published only after legal review, before any account system or data-collecting service launches.
