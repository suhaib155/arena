Glossary

The language of the MovenRun world, defined once.

Movement session
A recorded period of walking, running, or cycling — the fundamental action in MovenRun.
Proof of movement
A verifiable, deterministic proof that a session really happened, attested by the oracle so onchain contracts can act on it.
Zone
One hexagonal cell of the world map — the atom of territory. Every location belongs to exactly one zone.
Territory
The set of zones a player owns and maintains.
Capture
Claiming the zones a verified route crossed, turning them into owned territory.
Zone Deed
The onchain asset representing ownership of a zone — one deed per zone, held under the owner's keys, carrying provenance, defense, and yield.
Defense
A zone's resistance to being taken; it rises with active holding and fortification and is tested by challenges.
Fortify
An action that strengthens a zone you hold.
Challenge
The onchain contest by which one player competes for another's held zone; movement is weighed and the deed holds or changes hands.
District
A group of zones forming a neighborhood, with its own mastery and role in city competition.
City war
A season-long contest between clubs for control of a city's districts.
Club
A team of players who pool territory and standing to compete at district and city scale.
Crew mission
A shared, team-oriented objective for a club.
$MOVE
The network's native asset: minted by verified movement, used across the game, and burned by core actions.
Movement points
The in-game measure of your activity in a session.
Season
A recurring period of competition with its own objectives, standings, and rewards.
Oracle
The service that attests verified proofs of movement onchain, bridging real-world activity to the protocol.
Vault
The staking and community-treasury contract — the network's economic reserve.
Governance
Onchain, timelock-secured proposals and voting through which the community controls the network.
Timelock
A mandatory delay between a governance decision passing and taking effect, giving the community time to react.
H3
The open hexagonal grid system MovenRun uses to tile the world into zones.
Embedded wallet
A secure wallet created for a player automatically, so ownership works without seed phrases or setup.
Sponsored gas
Transaction fees paid on the player's behalf, so no one needs to hold a gas token to play.
Base
The Ethereum Layer 2 network MovenRun settles on.
