MovenRun documentation

MovenRun turns walking, running, and cycling into a motivating territory game. You move through your real city, nearby zones come to life on the map, and consistency — not extreme performance — is what makes your territory grow. A responsible, optional Web3 ownership layer is being explored for later, and is never required to play.

In three sentences. MovenRun is a health-first movement game that makes your city feel like a playground. Today it is a development-stage Android beta where the full territory loop runs as an on-device experience, backed by real verification infrastructure and experimental testnet contracts. Nothing in MovenRun requires a wallet, promises rewards, or represents real-world land.

The core loop

  1. Move — walk, run, or cycle at any pace. A session records distance, time, and route.
  2. Capture — your route brings nearby zones to life and claims territory.
  3. Return — territory softens over time; revisiting favorite routes keeps it strong and builds streaks.
  4. Grow — XP, collections, districts, clubs, and city play deepen as your routine holds.

Product principles

  • Health first. Consistency and participation beat intensity. No medical claims, ever.
  • Honest status. Every feature in these docs carries a status badge — what is real today is never blurred with what is planned.
  • Privacy-minded. Movement summaries over raw location trails; see Privacy & movement data.
  • Web3 is optional. The core experience needs no wallet and no purchase. Ownership experiments stay on testnet behind release gates.
  • No financial promises. In-app progress is not a payout, a token, or an investment.
