Our approach

Useful, understandable, and never forced.

Web3 should make selected ownership records more transparent and portable. It should not add friction to a morning walk or turn movement into a financial promise.

  • ✓Core play without a wallet
  • ✓Clear language and user choice
  • ✓Privacy-minded movement design
  • ✓Transparent digital records
  • ✓Gradual, test-led rollout
  • ✓No speculative reward promises

Roadmap

Building the experience
in the right order.

Health, trust, and a strong movement loop come first. Social and ownership layers deepen only after the foundation works well.

Phase 1

Health-first movement beta

Reliable sessions, route summaries, goals, streaks, and territory progress with no wallet required.

Phase 2

Clubs and city challenges

Friendly group goals, local leaderboards, shared territory, and stronger community motivation.

Phase 3

Optional Zone Deed pilot

A carefully tested Base-powered ownership layer for users who choose deeper territory participation.

Phase 4

City and partner expansion

More communities, local events, meaningful partnerships, and new ways to make movement rewarding.
